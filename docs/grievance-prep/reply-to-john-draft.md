## Notes on this draft (not part of the email)

- **"Without prejudice"** — settlement discussions are normally held on this
  basis so they can't be used against you in the open processes. John will
  know the mechanics; the draft just flags that you understand the exit talk
  and the grievance are separate tracks.
- The email deliberately commits you to nothing. It keeps the grievance
  option fully open (leverage) while setting the destination.
- If John suggests waiting before signalling openness to a deal, take that
  advice — timing the approach is exactly what he's for. Sometimes it is
  better to let the grievance land first.
